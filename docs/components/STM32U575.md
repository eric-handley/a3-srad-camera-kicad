# STM32U575RGT6 Microcontroller

## Overview
STMicroelectronics STM32U575RGT6 Ultra-Low-Power MCU
- ARM Cortex-M33 @ 160 MHz (max)
- 512 KB Flash, 192 KB SRAM
- Ultra-low-power modes: Shutdown, Standby, Stop 2, Run
- Multiple I2C, UART, SPI interfaces

## Power Requirements

### VDD Rail (Main Digital Supply)
- **Voltage:** 3.3V (nominal), range 1.71V - 3.6V
- **Standby Current (Stop 2 Mode):** 4.0 µA (typical) @ 3.3V, 25°C
  - With RTC: Add ~0.53 µA
  - Temperature effects: 4-9 µA (25-85°C)
- **Record Current (Active Operation @ 12 MHz MSI):**
  - Core: 0.62 mA (typical), 1.30 mA (max) @ 3.3V, 25°C
  - Peripherals (I2C + UART): ~0.1-0.2 mA
  - **Total: 0.8-1.5 mA**

### VDDA Rail (Analog Supply)
- **Voltage:** 3.3V (tied to VDD), range 1.58V - 3.6V
- **Standby Current:** <10 µA (ADC/DAC/COMP not used)
- **Record Current:** <10 µA (minimal, no analog peripherals active)

### VDDUSB Rail (USB Supply - Not Used)
- **Voltage:** Tied to ground (USB not used in this design)

### VDDIO2 Rail (Port G I/O Supply - Not Used)
- **Voltage:** Tied to VDD or ground (Port G not used)

### VBAT Rail (Backup Domain - Optional)
- **Voltage:** 3.3V (optional backup battery connection)
- **Current:** ~0.53 µA (if RTC active)

## Operating Modes Summary

### Shutdown Mode (Deepest Sleep)
- **VDD Current:** 160 nA (typical) @ 25°C
- **Description:** Lowest power, RTC optional, SRAM contents lost except backup domain
- **Wake-up:** External interrupt pins

### Standby Mode (Very Low Power with BOR)
- **VDD Current:** 0.64 µA (typical, no IWDG) @ 3.3V, 25°C
- **VDD Current:** 1.23 µA (typical, with IWDG) @ 3.3V, 25°C
- **Temperature effects:** 1.69-13.7 µA (55-105°C)
- **Description:** BOR active, backup registers retained, SRAM2 optional retention
- **Wake-up:** RTC, external interrupts

### Stop 2 Mode (Low Power with Peripherals)
- **VDD Current:** 4.0 µA (typical) @ 3.3V, 25°C
- **Description:** LPDMA functional, suitable for intermittent I2C/UART communication
- **Wake-up:** I2C, UART, GPIO (IMU interrupt), timers
- **Use case:** Waiting for IMU data ready interrupt

### Run Mode (Active Operation)
Clock speeds and current consumption @ VDD=3.3V, 25°C:
- **4 MHz MSI:** 0.34 mA (typical), 0.77 mA (max)
- **12 MHz MSI:** 0.62 mA (typical), 1.30 mA (max)
- **24 MHz MSI:** 1.15 mA (typical), 2.30 mA (max)
- **160 MHz PLL:** 10.5 mA (typical @ 1.8V), 14.0 mA (max @ 30°C)

## Total Power Consumption

### Standby Mode (Stop 2)
- **VDD Rail:** 4.0 µA @ 3.3V
- **VDDA Rail:** <10 µA @ 3.3V
- **Total Power:** ~13-50 µW

### Record Mode (I2C/UART Active @ 12 MHz)
- **VDD Rail Core:** 0.62 mA (typical), 1.30 mA (max)
- **VDD Rail Peripherals:** ~0.15 mA (I2C + UART active)
- **VDDA Rail:** <0.01 mA
- **Total Current:** 0.8 mA (typical), 1.5 mA (max)
- **Total Power:** 2.6 mW (typical), 5.0 mW (max)

## Peripheral Capabilities
- **I2C:** Fast mode (400 kHz), Fast mode Plus (1 MHz)
- **UART:** Up to 12.5 Mbps
- **SPI:** Up to 80 Mbps
- **GPIO:** All I/O pins 5V tolerant (except analog pins)
- **IWDG:** Independent watchdog timer
- **RTC:** Real-time clock with calendar

## Maximum Current Ratings
- **Total VDD current (source):** 200 mA max
- **Per VDD pin current:** 100 mA max
- **I/O pin current:** ±8 mA (standard), ±20 mA (high drive)

## Notes
- Ultra-low-power design enables efficient supervisor operation
- Stop 2 mode balances low power with peripheral availability
- MSI clock (12 MHz) sufficient for I2C @ 400 kHz and UART @ 115200 baud
- Temperature range: -40°C to +85°C (industrial) or +105°C (automotive)
- Power consumption increases with temperature (especially in standby modes)
- VDDA can be tied to VDD when analog peripherals not used
- Internal voltage regulator can operate from 1.71-3.6V input
