# RK809-5 Power Management IC

## Overview
Rockchip RK809-5 PMIC
- Five fast-transient synchronous bucks (up to 2.5 A) with 2.5 MHz switching
- Nine software-programmable LDOs (0.6 V – 3.4 V) with 25 mV resolution
- Dual 2.1 A load switches with programmable current limit
- Integrated battery fuel gauge, audio codec (24-bit ADC/DAC), and RTC
- I2C-controlled sequencing and voltage programming
- Recommended VCC9 input range: 3.0 V – 5.5 V

## Input Power

### VCC9 (Main Input)
- **Voltage Range:** 3.0 V – 5.5 V (recommended operating conditions)
- **Power Dissipation:** up to 2 W (device total)
- **VB_OK Threshold:** 2.744 V / 2.8 V / 2.856 V (typical, VB_OK_SEL = 0b01; programmable 2.8 V – 3.6 V)
- **VB_UV Threshold:** 2.7 V typical (programmable 2.7 V – 3.4 V in 0.1 V steps)
- **VB_LO Threshold:** 3.2 V typical (programmable 2.8 V – 3.5 V)

### Auxiliary Inputs
- **VCC8 (Switch2 supply):** 2.7 V – 5.0 V
- **VCC5/VCC6/VCC7 (LDO supplies):** 2.0 V – 5.5 V depending on LDO group

## Buck Converters (High-Efficiency Switching)

### BUCK1
- **Input Supply:** VCC9 (2.7 V – 5.5 V)
- **Output Voltage Range:** 0.5 V – 2.4 V via I2C (12.5 mV steps for 0.5 V – 1.5 V, 100 mV steps for 1.5 V – 2.4 V)
- **Maximum Output Current:** 2.5 A
- **Load Transient:** 0.25 A → 2.5 A at 0.5 A/µs, VOUT = 1.0 V, droop ≈ 20 mV (L = 0.47 µH, COUT = 32 µF)
- **Switching Frequency:** 2.25 MHz (min) / 2.5 MHz (typ) / 2.75 MHz (max) when Vin – Vout > 1.5 V
- **Efficiency (Vin = 4.2 V, Vout = 1.0 V):** 75% @ 2 A, 82% @ 1 A, 85% @ 0.3 A

### BUCK2
- **Input Supply:** VCC9 (2.7 V – 5.5 V)
- **Output Voltage Range:** 0.5 V – 2.4 V via I2C (same step sizes as BUCK1)
- **Maximum Output Current:** 2.5 A
- **Load Transient:** 0.25 A → 2.5 A at 0.5 A/µs, VOUT = 1.0 V, droop ≈ 20 mV (L = 0.47 µH, COUT = 32 µF)
- **Switching Frequency:** 2.25 MHz – 2.75 MHz (typ 2.5 MHz)
- **Efficiency (Vin = 4.2 V, Vout = 1.0 V):** 75% @ 2 A, 82% @ 1 A, 85% @ 0.3 A

### BUCK3
- **Input Supply:** VCC9 (2.7 V – 5.5 V)
- **Output Voltage Options:**
  - Internal divider mode: 0.5 V – 2.4 V via I2C (typ 1.25 V; 12.5 mV/100 mV steps)
  - External resistor divider selectable (feedback default 0.8 V ± 0.016 V)
- **Maximum Output Current:** 1.5 A
- **Load Transient:** 0.15 A → 1.5 A at 0.5 A/µs, VOUT = 1.25 V, droop ≈ 17 mV (L = 0.47 µH, COUT = 22 µF)
- **Switching Frequency:** 2.25 MHz – 2.75 MHz (typ 2.5 MHz)
- **Efficiency (Vin = 4.2 V, Vout = 1.25 V):** 82% @ 1 A, 83% @ 0.3 A

### BUCK4
- **Input Supply:** VCC9 (2.7 V – 5.5 V)
- **Output Voltage Range:** 0.5 V – 3.4 V via I2C (12.5 mV steps to 1.5 V, 100 mV above)
- **Maximum Output Current:** 1.5 A
- **Load Transient:** 0.15 A → 1.5 A at 0.5 A/µs, VOUT = 3.0 V, droop ≈ 22 mV (L = 0.47 µH, COUT = 22 µF)
- **Switching Frequency:** 2.25 MHz – 2.75 MHz (typ 2.5 MHz)
- **Efficiency (Vin = 4.2 V, Vout = 3.0 V):** 92% @ 1 A, 93% @ 0.3 A

### BUCK5
- **Input Supply:** VCC9 (2.7 V – 5.5 V)
- **Output Voltage Range:** 1.5 V – 3.6 V via I2C (typ 3.3 V)
- **Maximum Output Current:** 2.5 A
- **Load Transient:** 0.25 A → 2.5 A at 0.5 A/µs, VOUT = 3.0 V, droop ≈ 40 mV (L = 0.47 µH, COUT = 44 µF)
- **Switching Frequency:** 1.8 MHz – 2.2 MHz (typ 2.0 MHz)
- **Efficiency (Vin = 4.2 V, Vout = 3.3 V):** 90% @ 2 A, 92% @ 0.3 A

## Load Switch Outputs

### Switch1 (SWOUT1)
- **Supply:** VCC9 (2.7 V – 5.0 V)
- **RDS(on):** ~90 mΩ
- **Programmable Current Limit:** 1.0 A, 1.5 A, 1.8 A, 2.1 A (SW1_ILIM[1:0])

### Switch2 (SWOUT2)
- **Supply:** VCC8 (2.7 V – 5.0 V)
- **RDS(on):** 100 mΩ (typ), 120 mΩ (max)
- **Programmable Current Limit:** 1.0 A, 1.5 A, 1.8 A, 2.1 A (SW2_ILIM[1:0])

## Power Consumption Summary
- **Shutdown Current (Isd):** 35 µA typ, 40 µA max
- **Power-On Current (all bucks/LDOs/ADC enabled, Iq1):** 1.9 mA typ
- **Power-On Current (BUCK1/4 + LDO1/2 only, Iq2):** 0.65 mA typ
- **Sleep Current (BUCK1/4 + LDO1/2 in low-power sleep, Isleep):** 0.19 mA typ

## Features
- 2.5 MHz switching for reduced external magnetics
- Programmable power-up/down sequencing via OTP/I2C
- Integrated battery fuel gauge with 16-bit voltage/current ADCs and OCV + Coulomb counting
- Audio subsystem: 24-bit ADC/DAC, headphone (class-AB) and speaker (class-D) drivers, I2S/PDM interface
- Real-time clock with 32.768 kHz crystal support
- Low standby current: 35 µA (shutdown)
