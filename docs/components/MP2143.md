# MP2143 Synchronous Buck Converter

## Overview
Monolithic Power Systems MP2143 step-down switcher
- 3A continuous output current with integrated power MOSFETs
- Constant on-time (COT) control for fast transient response
- 1.2 MHz default switching frequency (±20% tolerance)
- 40 µA quiescent current for high efficiency at light loads
- 80 mΩ high-side and 40 mΩ low-side internal MOSFETs
- Output voltage as low as 0.6 V with excellent load/line regulation

## Input Power

### VIN (Main Input)
- **Voltage Range:** 2.5 V – 5.5 V (operating), up to 6 V (absolute max)
- **Under-Voltage Lockout (UVLO):** 2.2 V typ rising, 150 mV hysteresis
- **Quiescent Current:** 40 µA typ (enabled, no load)
- **Shutdown Current:** 0.1 µA typ (EN = 0V)

## Buck Converter Performance

### Output Voltage
- **Voltage Range:** 0.6 V – (VIN - 0.5 V)
- **Feedback Reference:** 0.6 V ±1.5% (2.5V ≤ VIN ≤ 5.5V, 25°C)
- **Maximum Output Current:** 3A continuous
- **100% Duty Cycle:** Supported in dropout condition

### Switching Characteristics
- **Switching Frequency:** 1.2 MHz typ (±20% at 25°C, ±25% over temp)
- **ON Time (tON):** 200 ns typ (VIN = 5V, VOUT = 1.2V), 277 ns typ (VIN = 3.6V, VOUT = 1.2V)
- **Minimum OFF Time:** 50 ns typ
- **Soft-Start Time:** 1.3 ms typ
- **Soft-Stop Time:** 1 ms typ

### Internal MOSFETs
- **High-Side (PFET) RDS(ON):** 80 mΩ typ
- **Low-Side (NFET) RDS(ON):** 40 mΩ typ
- **Switch Leakage:** 0.1 µA typ, 2 µA max (disabled, VSW = 0V or 5V)

### Efficiency (VIN = 5V, VOUT = 1.2V, L = 1.0 µH)
- **At 3A:** ~75%
- **At 2A:** ~75%
- **At 1A:** ~82%
- **At 300 mA:** ~85%

### Efficiency (VIN = 3.3V, VOUT = 1.2V)
- **Peak efficiency:** ~90% at moderate loads
- **Light load:** Maintained high efficiency due to proprietary light-load control

### Load and Line Regulation
- **Load Regulation:** ±0.2% typ (VIN = 5.5V and 3.3V, 0A – 3A)
- **Line Regulation:** ±0.2% typ (2.5V – 5.5V, ILOAD = 1.5A and 3A)

## Protection Features

### Current Limit
- **High-Side Current Limit:** 4.2 A min, 4.8 A typ (cycle-by-cycle)
- **Protection Mode:** Hiccup mode on sustained over-current
- **Short-Circuit Protection:** Hiccup mode with automatic recovery

### Thermal Protection
- **Thermal Shutdown:** 150°C typ
- **Thermal Hysteresis:** 30°C typ

## Control Features

### Enable (EN)
- **Logic Low (Disable):** < 0.4 V
- **Logic High (Enable):** > 1.2 V
- **Input Current:** 2 µA typ (VEN = 2V), 0.1 µA typ (VEN = 0V)
- **Internal Pull-Down:** 1 MΩ to ground

### Power-Good (PG)
- **Upper Trip Threshold:** +10% of regulation voltage
- **Lower Trip Threshold:** -10% of regulation voltage
- **Delay:** 110 µs typ
- **Output Type:** Open-drain with 500 kΩ internal pull-up to VIN
- **Sink Current:** 1 mA max (VPG < 0.4V)
- **Logic High:** Pulled to VIN when output in regulation

## Light Load Operation
- Proprietary control scheme prevents switching node ringing
- Gradual LS-FET current ramp-down instead of immediate turnoff
- Improved efficiency without typical DCM ringing issues

## External Components

### Typical Application (1.2V @ 2A output)
- **Input Capacitor (C1):** 10 µF ceramic (X5R/X7R)
- **Output Capacitor (C2):** 10 µF – 22 µF ceramic (1206 or larger)
- **Inductor (L1):** 1.0 µH (recommended range: 0.82 µH – 4.7 µH)
- **Feedback Resistors:** R1 = 200 kΩ, R2 = 200 kΩ (for 1.2V output)

### Component Selection Guidelines
- **Inductor:** DC resistance < 15 mΩ for highest efficiency, ripple current ~30% of max load
- **Input Cap RMS Current:** > ILOAD/2 for worst case (VIN = 2×VOUT)
- **Output Cap:** Low-ESR ceramic preferred, stable with ceramic capacitors
- **Feedback Resistor R1:** 120 kΩ – 200 kΩ for stability

## Package
- **Package Type:** TSOT23-8
- **Thermal Resistance:** θJA = 100°C/W, θJC = 55°C/W (on JESD51-7 4-layer PCB)
- **Operating Junction Temp:** -40°C to +125°C

## Features
- Constant on-time control with input voltage feed-forward
- Fast transient response, easy loop stabilization
- Auto-discharge at power-off
- Stable with low-ESR ceramic output capacitors
- Cycle-by-cycle over-current protection
- EN and PG pins for power sequencing
- Minimal external component count
